"system edits"

Audio

2016


This piece explores digital audio feedback and what it adds to the texture of rhythmic and noise music. I put most of my focus on constructing dense textures of aggressive and pulsating sound patterns.

All sounds were created in a Max/MSP patch, including a kick and a snare and the various effects including degradation and reverb. The signals of the waveforms were running through a feedback delay, to which random parameters were assigned. I explored the different types of sound that this system could create and edited and layered them in Ableton. The piece moves forward in a more or less irrational way, discarding any recurring phrases or smooth transitions.
